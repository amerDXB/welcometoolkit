General Coverage Overview

Note for Dependents: If your dependents (spouse, children) meet the residency and status requirements, they generally need to apply separately but often share similar eligibility and waiting periods. Ensure you submit applications for all eligible family members.
Important: Private Insurance Needed! During any waiting period before provincial coverage starts, or if you are ineligible, it is crucial to have private health insurance to cover potential medical emergencies, which can be very expensive in Canada.
