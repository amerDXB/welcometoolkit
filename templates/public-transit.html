General Tips

  • Have your fare (card or cash) ready before boarding.
  • Let passengers exit before you board.
  • Move to the back of the bus/train to make space.
  • Offer priority seats (near doors) to seniors, people with disabilities, or those pregnant.
  • Keep bags off seats during busy times.
  • Use headphones for music/calls.

Accessibility

Transit systems offer accessibility features (ramps, designated seating, audio announcements). Check the official website for details.
