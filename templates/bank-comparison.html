Compare banking options and credit cards designed for newcomers to Canada. Find products to start building your financial foundation.

Disclaimer: Information presented here is for general guidance. Bank offers, fees, interest rates, and eligibility criteria change frequently. Always verify all details directly with the financial institution before applying for any product. This toolkit is not a financial advisor.
Building Credit History: Getting a Canadian credit card, even one with a small limit, and using it responsibly (making payments on time) is crucial for newcomers. It helps build your Canadian credit history, which is essential for future loans (car, mortgage), renting apartments, and sometimes even getting a cell phone plan.
